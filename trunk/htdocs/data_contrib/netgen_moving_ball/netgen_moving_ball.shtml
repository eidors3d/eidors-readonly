Contributed EIT Data:

Authors: Andy Adler, William R.B. Lionheart
Date: 2006.
Brief Description: Simulation of the movement of a small target moving in a 3D medium. Netgen is used to remesh the medium at each step.
License: Creative Commons Artistic License
Attribution Requirement: Not required
Format: Data are in the matlab v6 *.mat file format. Variables:
  • fmdl - forward model for simulation of the homogenous tank
  • homg_tank - measurements on the homogenous tank
  • target_spiral - target moving in a spiral starting at the centre
  • target_spirograph_fast - target moving in a 'spirograph' pattern (star shaped movement)
  • target_spirograph_slow - target moving slowly in a 'spirograph' (star shaped movement)
Methods: Circular Medium. 16 Electrodes numbered clockwise, with electrode #1 at the top. Adjacent stimulation and measurement. No measurement at the stimulation electrodes.
Data: Data (zip format)
